Horses 4k

Ambient Country — Cleveland, OH

Listen

Horses 4k is an experimental duo comprising Jacob Trombetta (pedal steel guitar and electronics) and Robin Guiler (electronics and synthesizer). They create evocative environmental ambient music inspired by the wide open plains of the American imagination.

Shrouded in processed field recordings, Trombetta’s oblique country licks intertwine with Guiler’s drones and glitches in a tapestry of cosmic Americana.

The duo of Jacob Trombetta and Robin Guiler feed pedal steel guitar into samplers and synthesizers, taking a distinctive instrument and manipulating it into something sometimes unrecognisable and otherworldly.
